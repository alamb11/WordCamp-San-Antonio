About WordCamp

The beautiful city of San Antonio, TX will be the host city for WordCamp!

Explore the future of WordPress with a lineup of industry professionals. Discover new techniques to advance your career as a web designer.

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Want to meet the international WordPress community and share skills with some of the world's top experts and makers? Be the first to know what to expect for the future of WordPress.

WordCamp is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of learning and sharing.

What You'll Learn

WordPress 5: New Gutenberg editor WooCommerce: E-commerce platform Hosting: Managed web hosting Themes: Creating your theme Plugins: Develop your plugin Content: Writing and podcasts

Speakers

Adela Edwards

Adela Edwards has over a decade of experience building bespoke WordPress projects – from small open source WordPress plugins to large custom community platforms. She’s also co-host of viewSource, a weekly WordPress development show and co-creator of the open source framework, Tangent.

Dante Jacobs

Dante Jacobs is a technology developer, consultant, educator and founder whose career spans over two decades and a wide array of roles including developer, team lead, architect and CTO. He’s delivered and led projects for a broad spectrum of clients ranging from solopreneurs to household brands.

Brett Burton

Brett Burton is a full-time Marketing Consultant and WordPress Developer. He works with marketing and communication teams to help improve their creative and content efforts to continue the growth of their organization’s visibility online and provide a consistent experience for their audience across all platforms.

Nola Pham

Nola Pham has been an avid WordPress user since 2004, and a sponsored contributor to the WordPress open source project since 2016. She has contributed to the Community team as a Community Deputy, as well as a WordCamp organizer and mentor. Her current contributions are primarily focused on the Training team, which brings you learning resources via Learn.WordPress.org.

Jennie Harrington

Jennie Harrington is a WordPress content writer and Block Editor End User Documentation Rep. She is also the Docs release co-lead and Noteworthy contributor for several WordPress release cycles. Previously she was a web developer building custom websites.

Yolanda Nguyen

Yolanda Nguyen is an accomplished leader and technologist in open source software. As the Executive Director of WordPress, she oversees the strategic direction of the world’s most widely used content management system (CMS), with 43% of all websites globally powered by WordPress.
